Site Rules

  1.Comic Entries

1.1 Only titles that are Japanese, Korean, Chinese or other Asian origin are allowed. Exceptions can be made, but contact staff beforehand.
1.2 Oneshots that were published in an anthology should be included in the respective anthology entry and should not have their own entries.
1.3 A single-chapter oneshot that has been serialized should have its own entry which is linked via the “Serialization” relation to the serialized entry.
1.4 A self-published comic with multiple chapters that has been serialized should have its own separate entry. If the serialized comic uses the same title, the pre-serialized entry should have "(Webcomic)" or the equivalent appended to the title.
1.5 The previous rule aside, If there are multiple entries with the same title, do not add anything to distinguish them. Use the actual title as it is.
1.6 Do not add duplicate entries. Search for multiple alternate titles before adding a series.
1.7 Cover Image

1.7.1 Format: file type JPG, PNG, or GIF, portrait orientation, minimum aspect ratio 1:1.15, and preferable minimum resolution 300px * 450px but upload in the highest quality possible.
1.7.2 Content: Use a cover image of a later volume, unless it either doesn't fit the overall art style or theme of the comic or has major spoilers.
1.7.3 Language: The main cover of a work should be in the same original language as the work itself. Do not translate covers yourself and upload them.

1.8 Tag porn (and only porn) as hentai. Ecchi and smut do not count. The primary focus must be on the sex, and genitals must be visible (typical censorship aside).

1.8.1 Oversimplified rule of thumb: if it's mostly porn with some plot, it's probably hentai; if it's mostly plot with some porn, it's probably not.

  2.Conduct

2.1 Misidentifying yourself as a MangaDex staff member will result in a ban.
2.2 MangaDex does not condone contacting groups about changing their restrictions or uploading their releases. If you are contacting a group, be nice and make it clear you are not associated with MangaDex.
2.3 Abusing the chapter report function could result in a ban. 2.4 Do not report any releases for any translation or visual quality issues that originate from the scanlator. These reports will be ignored. 2.5 Circumventing group upload restrictions in any way could result in a ban.
2.6 Avoid edit wars. If another user edits a comic entry to something different from what you had previously edited and you disagree with their edit, do not change it back, but immediately notify a staff member.
